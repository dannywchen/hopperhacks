## Usage Rules
- Primary CTA buttons: Gryffindor red background + gold text/border.
- Secondary actions: charcoal base + bronze border/text.
- Focus states: Ravenclaw bright blue outline for clarity and accessibility.
- Panels and frames: charcoal/mahogany surfaces with subtle bronze borders.
- Accent labels/chips: bronze/gold text on dark surfaces.

## Minimalism Principles
- Use one border per interactive element.
- Avoid stacked ornamental outlines unless required for hierarchy.
- Keep hover effects subtle: color shift over heavy shadows.
- Use house colors intentionally as semantic accents, not full-screen fills.
